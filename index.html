2. Information We Collect

Our application is designed with user privacy as a top priority:

  • Non-Personal Data: We may collect technical information such as device type (Smartphones, iPads, Tablets), operating system version, and app performance logs solely to optimize performance and user experience.
  • User Input Data: Any information you enter into the app (such as phone numbers or birth years for calculation) is processed locally on your device (Local Device Cache) and is not stored or transmitted to external servers.
